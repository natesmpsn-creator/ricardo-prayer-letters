Prayer Letter - June 2026

Dear Friends and Prayer Partners,

Greetings from Nicaragua! I want to share some wonderful updates from this month's work and ministry as we approach the mid-year mark.

Praise Points

Mother's Day Service (June 13) We held a special service celebrating the mothers in our ministry. It was a beautiful time of honor and recognition for these dedicated women serving alongside us in the work of the kingdom.

Church Property Improvements By God's grace, we are seeing wonderful progress on our church property. A new on-ramp to the property is currently under construction, and our main auditorium has been beautifully upgraded with a new audio-visual booth. We are grateful for those who gave toward these improvements and pray they will enhance our ministry capacity for years to come.

Sign Language Ministry One of our church members has been learning sign language and recently had the privilege of leading someone to Christ using sign language. This is a powerful example of how the Lord opens doors when we are willing to learn and step out in faith to reach those around us.

Upcoming Events

Vacation Bible School (VBS) In the second week of July, we will be hosting Vacation Bible School, and we are expecting hundreds of children to participate. This is an exciting opportunity to reach the younger generation with the Gospel.

National Teen Conference For the past 7 years, the Lord has allowed us to hold a National Teen Conference with the purpose of investing in and leading teenagers to grow in their walk with the Lord and commit their lives to following Christ. Over the years, we have witnessed many teens come to know the Lord, others baptized, and many more surrender to serve Him.

This year, God willing, we will be hosting close to 300 teenagers during the second week of July. The goals for the conference are: - To hear powerful preaching and teaching of God's Word - To experience Christ-centered fellowship - To participate in activities that strengthen faith and commitment - To see many come to know Christ and dedicate their lives to Him's service
